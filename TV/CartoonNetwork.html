Cartoon Network

Cartoon Network is a television channel known for animated content. It's valuable for society as it provides entertaining and often educational shows, contributes to cultural impact with iconic characters, fosters creativity in animation, promotes positive values, offers family-friendly entertainment, and serves as a platform for emerging talent in the industry.

Most iconic shows of CN

Adventure Time

"Adventure Time" is an animated series following the adventures of Finn and Jake in the Land of Ooo. Filled with whimsical quests, magical characters, and a unique blend of fantasy and humor, it has captivated audiences with its imaginative storytelling and quirky charm.

Author: Pendleton Ward

No. of seasons: 10

No. of episodes: 283

Release: April 5, 2010 – September 3, 2018

Ben 10 (2005)

"Ben 10" follows the adventures of Ben Tennyson, a boy with the Omnitrix that lets him transform into different aliens. With his cousin Gwen and Grandpa Max, Ben battles villains, providing a blend of action and sci-fi.

Author: Man of Action Entertainment

No. of seasons: 4

No. of episodes: 49

Release: December 27, 2005 – April 15, 2008

Related:
Ben 10: Alien Force,
Ben 10: Ultimate Alien,
Ben 10: Omniverse,
Ben 10 (2016)

Regular Show

"Regular Show" follows the adventures of Mordecai and Rigby, two friends working as park groundskeepers. Filled with offbeat humor and surreal situations, the series blends everyday workplace scenarios with fantastical elements and quirky characters.

Author: J. G. Quintel

No. of seasons: 8

No. of episodes: 261

Release: September 6, 2010 – January 16, 2017
