Welcome to Animation Wiki!

Animation has allowed storytellers to tell stories in unique ways. The visceral fantastical worlds in animated films can reignite inspiration and the magic of stories no matter what age. So if you’re a storyteller, or want to be, the animation is a great place to start. So what is animation? And what are the types of animation cartoonists and artists use? Let’s jump in.

Animation can explain whatever the mind of man can conceive. This facility makes it the most versatile and explicit means of communication yet devised for quick mass appreciation.

Walt Disney

Movies

These are standalone works typically lasting anywhere from 70 minutes to over two hours. They are designed to tell a complete story within a single film.

Series

These are episodic and designed for television. They consist of multiple episodes, usually ranging from 20 to 30 minutes each. The story unfolds gradually over the course of multiple episodes or seasons.
